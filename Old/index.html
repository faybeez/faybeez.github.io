What are SI Base Units?

  • Standard units of measurement
  • Defined by the International System of Units
  • A basic set from which other SI units can be derived
  • 7 SI Base Units:
Unit Physical Quantity Symbol
meter length m
kilogram mass kg
second time s
ampere electric current A
kelvin temperature K
mole amount of a substance mol
candela luminous intensity cd
